About Us

Stephanie founded Maid To Clean with a simple vision: to provide reliable, detail-oriented cleaning services that make a real difference in people's lives. With years of experience in the cleaning industry, she recognized a need for trustworthy, professional service backed by genuine care for customers. Stephanie's commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind Maid To Clean's success. She personally oversees every aspect of the business, from hiring and training staff to ensuring that every job meets her high standards. Stephanie's hands-on approach and dedication to quality have earned Maid To Clean a loyal customer base and a reputation for exceptional service.
