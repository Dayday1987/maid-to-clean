About Maid To Clean

Our Story

Maid To Clean was born from a simple belief: every home deserves care, and every community deserves support. What started as one person's determination to provide honest, thorough cleaning services has grown into a trusted team dedicated to transforming spaces and building lasting relationships.

Our founder, Stephanie, began this journey with nothing but grit, attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to doing things right. That spirit—what we call the "Stephanie Standard"—still guides everything we do today.

Our Core Values

These serve as the "Maid To Clean" promise—the principles that guide every visit, every interaction, and every decision we make.

People Over Profit

We measure our success by the strength of our relationships and the smiles on our clients' faces, not just the numbers on a ledger.

Uncompromising Integrity

We treat every home and office with the utmost respect, ensuring that honesty and transparency are at the heart of every visit.

Community-Centric Service

We don't just work here; we live here. We are dedicated to lifting up our neighbors and investing in the local community that supports us.

The "Stephanie Standard"

Inspired by our founder's beginnings, we approach every task with the same grit, detail, and dedication that started it all.
