Meet Stephanie

Stephanie is the heart behind Maid To Clean. A neighbor, a mother, and a lifelong member of this community, she understands the invisible weight a cluttered home can place on a busy life.

She didn't start this business to build an empire—she started it because she saw people overwhelmed. Families juggling too much. Homes becoming another source of stress.

What began as a simple idea became a calling: to create a service that doesn't just clean, but gives people room to breathe again.

From a Problem to a Purpose

Modern life doesn't slow down. The to-do list keeps growing, and something always gets pushed aside.

Stephanie saw families missing moments that matter—not because they wanted to, but because they were overwhelmed.

Maid To Clean was built on a simple belief: a clean home isn't a luxury—it is the foundation for peace, clarity, and time well spent.
