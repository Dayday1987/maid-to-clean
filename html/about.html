About Maid To Clean

Built on integrity, driven by excellence—your trusted partner for professional cleaning services.

Meet Stephanie Kirkley

Stephanie founded Maid To Clean with a simple vision: to provide reliable, detail-oriented cleaning services that make a real difference in people's lives. With years of experience in the cleaning industry, she recognized a need for trustworthy, professional service backed by genuine care for customers.

What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of dedicated cleaning professionals, each hand-selected and trained to uphold Stephanie's high standards. Every member of our team undergoes thorough background checks and receives comprehensive training in proper cleaning techniques, safety protocols, and customer service excellence.

Today, Stephanie continues to oversee every aspect of the business, ensuring that each client receives the personalized attention and exceptional results that have become our hallmark.
